## 💥 What Is This?

A browser-based slingshot game where you fling Furbies at targets across **400 procedurally escalating levels**. Every hit triggers a full explosion with shockwave rings, flying debris, fire sparks, and screen flash.

---

## 🕹️ How to Play

| Control | Action |
|--------|--------|
| **Click & Hold** near the slingshot | Charge your shot |
| **Drag back** | Aim — further = more power |
| **Release** | Launch the Furby! |
| **🔄 Restart** | Reset anytime |
| **🔊 Sound** | Toggle audio |
